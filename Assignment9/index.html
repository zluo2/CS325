Car accident

  • High Concept: Avoiding the car accident and get the high score.
  • Theme: Motor racing.
  • Mandated Variety: Just using the mouse control.
  • Player Experience Goals: I hope my players can feel exciting and interesting when they are playing my game.
  • Gameplay: This game is very easy, you just need to use your mouse to control motor to get high score and avoid car accident.
  • Prototype Goal: Racing.
  • Features: You can use the mouse control the mouse to any place in map.
  • Setting/Premise: Good luck
  • Story: No having fun
  • Target Audience: Someone who like motor racing.
  • Play Time: 3-5 mins
  • Strategies: I think the main way to play my game is saving more time possibility. Because the game only has five mins, the player need to finish the game (arriving the destination) before the time up. Mainly, some events would reduce your time. Therefore, the player needs to calculate how to use their limit time effectively
